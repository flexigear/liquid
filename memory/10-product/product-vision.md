# Product Vision

## Experience Goal

The game should feel tactile, calm, and mesmerizing first, then clever as puzzle structure is layered on top.

## Product Identity

This is not mainly a rolling-ball puzzle game. It is a container-liquid interaction game where the vessel shape, material behavior, and inertia form the main appeal.

## Differentiators

- Transparent 3D vessel as the full play space.
- Physically credible liquid with free-surface motion.
- Water drops that merge into larger bodies while remaining visually readable.
- Puzzle progression built from vessel-specific behavior.

## Emotional Targets

- "I want to swirl it again just to watch it."
- "That moved exactly like water should."
- "I solved the container, not just a level."

## Design Bias

- Strong sensory believability over high rule count.
- Small number of high-quality interactions over broad feature spread.
- Vessel identity should matter as much as liquid identity.
